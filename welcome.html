Welcome to the XSCALE Alliance

We're grateful for your support!

What does it mean to be involved in XSCALE Alliance?

We're a network of mutual benefit for independent coaches and consultancies working to make the Agile Organization a reality. To this end we're working together on training courses, events, books, tools, podcasts and videos; if you're working in DevOps, Product Managmement, or Business Agility, we'll be delighted to get you involved in XSCALE.

  • Blueprint

We're actively seeking coaches, stewards and partners worldwide to take part in the XSCALE learning ecosystem. Only qualified coaches contribute to our training collateral, but we welcome the entire agile world to contribute to the XSCALE Blueprint, which can always benefit from refactoring, updating and illustration. If you're looking to contribute there we're always delighted by pull-requests.

When will my signature be posted on the xscalealliance.org site?

We still have to automate this so there may be some delay due to volume. We may not be able to respond to all comments or inquiries promptly for this same reason.
